# Teamcenter Flow Notes

## Part Creation Flow

1. Open Teamcenter.
2. Authenticate successfully.
3. Wait for the home shell to become interactive.
4. Open the Part Creation feature from the navigation or launcher.
5. Start a new part record.
6. Populate the mandatory part fields.
7. Save the record and verify a success acknowledgement.
8. Use Teamcenter search to locate the new part.
9. Confirm the new part appears in the results.

## Search Considerations

- Teamcenter shells can be slow after save operations, so prefer visible-state and response-based waits.
- Search may be available as a global search box, a toolbar field, or a dedicated results panel depending on the deployment.
- Avoid relying on dynamic DOM IDs generated by Teamcenter components.
